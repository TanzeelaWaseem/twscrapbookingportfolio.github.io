Scrapbooking is a crafting activity which invloves collecting pictures, stickers, stamps etc and decorating them on blank pages. This activity has no bound of rules, therefore it is very easy to be done by people of any age. It is a fun and relaxing activity which can be enjoyed in isolation or with a friend(s) or family.

There are bunch of themes that can be implemented when scrapbooking - the ones that I have focused on are color-based and vintage themed. My portfolio will be presenting my scrapbooking work as well as some digital wallpapers generated through AI that you can download and use.
